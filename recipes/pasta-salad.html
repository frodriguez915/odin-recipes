Pasta Salad

Description

Makes for a good healthy meal. Is easy to make and can be prepped for a later eating.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound tri-colored spiral pasta
  • 16 ounce bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 8 tablespoons salad seasoning mix
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 2 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, chopped

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil
  2. Whisk Italian dressing and salad spice mix together until smooth. Combine pasta, tomatoes, bell peppers, and olives in a salad bowl.
  3. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat.
  4. Refrigerate salad, 8 hours to overnight.
