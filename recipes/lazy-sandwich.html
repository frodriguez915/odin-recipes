Lazy Sandwich

Description

For when you're feeling lazy. A basic sandwich recipe will do just the trick.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 3 pieces of sliced turkey
  • 1 spoonfull of mayonaise
  • 2 pieces of lettuce
  • 4 slices of tomato
  • 1 slice of cheese

Steps

  1. Get one of the slices of bread and rub the spoonfull of mayonaise on one side
  2. Place the cheese
  3. Place a piece of lettuce
  4. Place all the piece of sliced turkey
  5. Place the remaining lettuce
  6. Place all the tomatoes
  7. Top it all off with the remaining slice of bread
